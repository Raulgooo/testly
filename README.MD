# Testly: Duolingo for Anything

## What does it do?
**Testly has been created with the sole purpose of helping students who struggle selecting what and when to study when picking from a textbook. Its design is inspirated by sports periodization and Science-Backed learning, helping the student to reach peak performance just before exams.**

## How does it do it?
**LLM powered core that analyzes your PDF textbook and thoroughly extracts chapters, sections and topics to analyze their content, difficulty, time to complete and distribute it across your calendar, which is made taking into account your study variables as time and days available, course duration, exam weeks and more on the future.**
